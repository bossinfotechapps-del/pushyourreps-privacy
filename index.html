Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 1, 2026

1. Introduction

Boss Infotech ("we", "us", or "our") operates the Push Your Reps - Fitness mobile application ("App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our App.

By using Push Your Reps, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

2. Information We Collect

Personal Information (via Google Sign-In):

  • Name and email address (for account creation and cloud sync)
  • Profile photo (from your Google account)

Health & Fitness Data (stored locally and in Firebase):

  • Workout logs (exercises, sets, reps, weights)
  • Body measurements (weight, body fat, chest, waist, bicep, thigh)
  • Nutrition logs (meals, calories, protein, carbs, fat)
  • Water intake tracking
  • Sleep tracking (bed time, wake time, hours, quality)
  • Fitness goals and preferences
  • Gym session times (start time, end time, duration)

Device Information:

  • Device type and operating system version
  • App version

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide personalized workout tracking and fitness insights
  • To sync your data across devices via Firebase Cloud
  • To calculate and display your fitness statistics and progress
  • To provide AI-powered coaching recommendations
  • To display relevant advertisements via Google AdMob
  • To improve the App's features and user experience

4. Data Storage & Security

  • Local Storage: On your device using AsyncStorage for offline access
  • Cloud Storage: In Google Firebase Firestore (encrypted in transit and at rest)

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data. However, no method of electronic storage is 100% secure.

5. Third-Party Services

  • Google Firebase — Authentication, Cloud Firestore (data sync)
  • Google AdMob — Advertisement display
  • Google Sign-In — Account authentication

Each third-party service has its own privacy policy. We encourage you to review their policies.

6. Advertisements

Push Your Reps displays advertisements through Google AdMob. AdMob may use cookies and device identifiers to serve personalized ads. You can opt out of personalized advertising through your device settings.

7. Data Retention

  • Free users: Data retained for 15 days in cloud
  • Premium users: Data retained for 2+ years in cloud
  • Local data: Retained until you uninstall or clear app data

8. Data Deletion

You can delete your account and all data at any time:

  • Go to Profile → Account → Delete Account
  • This permanently deletes all workout history, nutrition logs, measurements, and profile data from device and cloud
  • This action cannot be undone

You may also request data deletion by emailing us.

9. Children's Privacy

Push Your Reps is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

10. Your Rights

  • Access your personal data stored in the App
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt out of personalized advertisements
  • Export your data

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you by posting the new policy and updating the "Last updated" date.

12. Health Disclaimer

Push Your Reps is for informational and fitness tracking purposes only. It is NOT medical advice. Consult a healthcare provider before starting any exercise program. Use at your own risk.
