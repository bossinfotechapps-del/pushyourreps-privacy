Account Deletion Request

Last updated: March 2026

1. Requesting Account Deletion

Users of Push Your Reps - Fitness can permanently delete their account and associated data at any time.

You can request account deletion using the following methods:

  • Inside the app: Profile → Account → Delete Account
  • By emailing us at bossinfotech.apps@gmail.com

2. What Data Will Be Deleted

When your account is deleted, the following data will be permanently removed from our systems:

  • User profile information
  • Workout history and exercise logs
  • Nutrition and meal tracking data
  • Body measurements
  • Sleep tracking data
  • Water intake tracking
  • Fitness goals and preferences
  • Cloud backup data stored in Firebase

3. Data Retention Period

After a deletion request is received, all associated account data will be removed from our systems within:

7 days

Backup systems may retain limited encrypted data for a short period before automatic deletion.

4. Data Stored on Your Device

Some fitness data may remain stored locally on your device until you:

  • Uninstall the app
  • Clear the app storage

5. Third-Party Services

Push Your Reps uses the following services which may temporarily process data:

  • Google Firebase (Authentication & Cloud Firestore)
  • Google AdMob (Advertisements)
